Create a simple invoice quickly in just a few steps. To start, hit the “Invoice” button on your home screen, and start adding client details. Add the items – including quantity, pricing, and any discounts that you want added – set your payment terms, and hit “Next” to preview your fast, professional-looking invoice. Once you’re ready, you can send to your customer via email, text message, or any other messaging option that your mobile device offers.
